Eligibility:

This contest is exclusively for the students currently studying in IIIT, Kalyani. For successful completion of the contest, project completion/adequate progress is mandatory.



Everyone else:

Getting 2 pull requests merged in any of the organizations. This is not strictly necessary, but ensure that your contributions stand out in terms of quality rather than quantity.

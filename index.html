Eligibility:

This contest is for all under-graduate students. For successful completion of the contest, project completion/adequate progress is mandatory.



Everyone else:

Getting 2 pull requests merged in any of the organizations. This is not strictly necessary, but ensure that your contributions stand out in terms of quality rather than quantity.

FAQ

Click on the Question for Answer

  • How to register?
  • Click on the register button. A form will open where you need to put in your details. In the project domain, select the checkbox against the technical stack you want to explore in the coming month. If you have a specific project idea, you can tell us about in in short or detail, as you please!
  • Is it compulsory for student applicants to have a project an idea?
  • No, you are free to leave that empty if you are just getting started. However, we would love to know your ideas!
  • In the proposed project we have to write one of our projects or select from the 5 on the website?
  • You can do either. A lot of projects will be added once the mentors decide on the project ideas. In case you do not submit any ideas, the selection committee will assign you a project and a mentor. The project will be based on one of your selected technology domains.
  • Number of people in a team?
  • The number of students in a team will be maximum 2. Each team will be working on a single project, along with a mentor who will guide them.
